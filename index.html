Deloitte Digital Bike Demo

This is a demo by Deloitte Digital to explore frontend innovation with the goals to create a highly immersive and interactive e-commerce product page.

The lines between web, VR/AR and shop experience are getting blurry. Product images on a website could be much interactive using WebGL technology which is currently part of all modern browsers! Products can be shown in 3D in an interactive way. Potential buyers can explore products and learn about its features.

On top of that this technology can also be used to show content in virtual reality to create an even more immersive experience using your smartphone in combination with cardboard of plastic VR glasses.

The same content could be used enhance the shopping experience in a store to experience the product and features. Dedicated VR/AR glasses could for example give you the experience of using this bike and gear in the French Alps.
